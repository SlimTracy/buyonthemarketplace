Ultimate Guide To Buying and Selling On Facebook Marketplace

Yes, it's entirely possible to turn your unused items into a consistent revenue stream right from your living room. However, this involves shifting gears from the common advice out there about selling on online platforms.

Just last week, an aspiring seller from our community reached out to me, asking for some "tricks" to quickly sell items on Facebook Marketplace. I responded with an enthusiastic "Sure, I'd love to help!" But here's the twist - instead of offering run-of-the-mill tips, I emphasized the power of high-quality photos and understanding your buyers and sellers.

The reply? "Oh, that seems too much work for just selling my stuff." It's understandable, given that most advice tends to revolve around posting items for sale and waiting for buyers. But let me tell you, there's more to it than that, and it's not about cutting corners or taking the path of least resistance. It's about maximizing your returns.

No wonder many people find selling online frustrating. And no wonder they are taken aback when I say, “Hey, instead of simply listing your item, let's make it stand out. Let me show you how to enhance its appeal."

They started with a post like this one... and decided to revolutionize their selling experience. Our Facebook Marketplace students have had their journey from simply listing to selling like pros.

Take a lesson from them: If you can make one remarkable sale, you can make three. Then 10. You can turn your unused stuff into a consistent revenue source.

Take Jenna, a student from our program who started with selling old DVDs and ended up creating a sustainable income source by just selling on Facebook Marketplace.

Let's delve into some of the things she and others learned and perfected over time:

  1. Great Photos are Everything: Your phone probably houses a great camera. Utilize it. A picture speaks a thousand words - make sure your product photo is shouting "Buy me!" at the top of its lungs.
  2. Lighting Matters: Good lighting can transform your product from "meh" to "wow." Don't just take photos; take captivating ones. Remember, your item's first impression can make or break the sale.
  3. Product Clean-up: An item in its best condition can fetch a better price. Dust off that old lamp or polish that silverware before you snap a picture. A little effort can add some extra bucks to your selling price.
  4. Unique Photos: Showcase different angles – Front, Side, Back, Other side. It not only provides a comprehensive view of the product but also builds trust with your potential buyers.
  5. Seal the Deal and Leave: No lingering after the transaction. A swift exit avoids unnecessary complications (like the guy who stole money out of my car after a sale because I didn't roll up my windows).
  6. Know Your Seller/Buyer: Do your homework. Check their profiles, read reviews. Understanding your buyer or seller can save you from potential scams and ensure smooth transactions.

Implement these strategies and watch your Facebook Marketplace game change. Remember, if one person can do it, so can you. It's time to dust off those unused items and start selling like a pro.

Take the plunge, embrace the change, and make Facebook Marketplace your playground.
